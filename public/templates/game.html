Instructions:

  • Use the cursor () keys to move your character
  • Your actions affect your world, the other player's world, or both.
  • The other player only "sees" what you "show" them.
  • Use the appropriate color when drawing messages:
    • Blue: "I can do this" or "You should do this."
    • Yellow: "I see something" or "Is this different?"
    • Red: "I can't go there" or "I can't do that."
  • Messages are limited to 8 lines. Words aren't feasible.
  • Your success depends on effective communication.
